About
THE ACADEMY

Lions Cricket Academy is one of Rajasthan's most respected multi-sport institutions, based in Rangbari, Kota. We combine NIS certified coaching with genuine personal attention to unlock every athlete's true potential.

Our programs span Cricket, Archery, Self Defence and Fitness — structured for all levels, from absolute beginners to those preparing for district and state competition.

  • All age groups — beginner to advanced
  • NIS & SAI certified professional coaching
  • Small batches for personalised attention
  • Morning & evening sessions available
  • Regular match practice & tournaments
  • Four disciplines under one roof
